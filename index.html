Designed for real life

  • Fast daily check‑ins – complete habits with a single tap, or record minutes/pages.
  • Streaks & score – see your day at a glance with a simple score and streak levels.
  • Smart suggestions – Habitforge notices when a goal is too heavy and suggests adjusting it.
  • Widgets & live timers – keep habits visible on your home screen and lock screen.
  • No account needed – everything works offline; optionally sync via your own backups.

For makers who love focus

  • Category based templates – sport, health, reading, routines, quit goals and more.
  • Difficulty & XP – harder habits give more XP so your effort is rewarded.
  • Stats & heatmaps – see which days and times you are most consistent.
  • Thoughtful notifications – quiet hours, gentle reminders, and countdown timers.

Privacy first

Habitforge is built so that your data stays on your device by default. We don’t require an account or email to use the app.

  • Habits, completions, notes and stats are stored locally on your phone.
  • Notifications are scheduled on your device; we don’t send push notifications from our servers.
  • You can export your data at any time from Settings.

See the full Privacy Policy for more details.
